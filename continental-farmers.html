Continental Farmers Group
Since 2012,  SALIC acquired two farming companies over multiple stages and has successfully consolidated them under Continental Farmers Groups (CFG) by the end of 2018.
CFG is now one of the largest farming companies in Ukraine, where it manages 195,000 hectares of high-quality arable land located around Ternopil area (West Ukraine)
The annual production is around one million tons of Wheat, Barley, Corn, Soybean, OSR, Potatoes, and Sugar Beet with total grain storage capacity of 420,000 tons
Moreover, CFG is integrating technology with its farming operations to ensure efficient and sustainable farming in the coming years.
