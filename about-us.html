About SALIC

SALIC (Saudi Agricultural and Livestock Investment Company) was founded by Royal Decree No. 22 on 18/04/1430 H 14/04/2009, as a Saudi joint stock company owned by the Public Investment Fund, and its statute stipulated that its activity in the field of agricultural investment and livestock production in countries with comparative advantages, To contribute to the provision of food commodities that are invested in the production and supply of the countries targeted by the investment in order to contribute to the achievement of food security and the provision of food products and price stability in the Kingdom. To achieve this, the company has the right to set up subsidiaries on its own or through partnership with national companies, professional investors and regional and international organizations in the Kingdom or abroad.
SALIC (Saudi Agricultural and Livestock Investment Company was launched in 2012 with the aim of entering all the links of the agricultural investment chain in agricultural projects and livestock production as a company specializing in agricultural investment and livestock production.
SALIC is seeking investment opportunities in countries that welcome foreign investment and enjoy comparative advantage over added value. SALIC's investments contribute to enhancing agricultural production and improving efficiency, as well as developing competencies in the agricultural investment chain.
Our investments contribute to the provision of targeted food supplies and we are working hard to implement our ambitious strategy to partner with governments, companies and communities of expertise. We at SALIC are keen to invest in agricultural projects and livestock production projects and establish these projects, in addition to following up the implementation of these agreements with local and international agricultural companies and with all investors in the agricultural investment chain.
The company's strategy aims to focus on commodities: wheat, barley, corn grains, soybeans, rice, sugar, vegetable oils, green feed, red meat, poultry and dairy, aquaculture.
SALIC is actively investing in agricultural investment and livestock production, and the management is supported by objectives and profit-seeking within the company's strategy.
